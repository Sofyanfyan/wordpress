Hometown description

Gresik is a city located north of Surabaya. Part of the province of East Java. It is an area that has a lot of history and traditions.

Culture of Gresik City Gresik is known for its culture that continues from generation to generation, for example the milkfish market and the slawe night. The milkfish market is a tradition or activity of the Gresik community to welcome Eid al-Fitr, but those who celebrate are not only the Gresik people but also people from neighboring cities, such as Lamongan, Sidoarjo, Mojokerto and surrounding cities.

And in addition to the milkfish market tradition, there is also the night selawe tradition that has been going on for a long time and it is not known when it took place, and the community has carried on this tradition until now, and this tradition is besides night to look for lailatul kaddar, malam selawe is also used by pilgrims to make pilgrimages to Tomb of Sunan Giri, and there are many people from various regions who come, and also from various ages, this event is held on the 24th day of Ramadan before the 25th night.

Each city has its own characteristics, and the city of Gresik has its own characteristics that everyone from all over who has traveled to Gresik city has known, that is, within the scope of this city there are always many kinds of industries, from cottage industries to industries. Pertamina which is already international, and the most striking industry there is Semen Gresik, whose products are already used by all Indonesian people, therefore the city of Gresik is usually called an industrial city. Apart from having the characteristics of the industrial city of Gresik, it is also often called the city of students and the city of Pudak, especially when Gresik is currently adding new facilities which are still in the development process, such as an international port and stadium.
