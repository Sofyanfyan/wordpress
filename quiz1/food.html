Gresik City Foods

  1. Pudak

    2. Pudak or ope is a typical Gresik cake. The taste of the food is guaranteed delicious and addicting. This food is made from coconut milk, brown sugar and flour rice.The ingredients are then mixed until evenly distributed, then wrapped in banana leaves then steamed until cooked. Pudak served warm, you can eat it and enjoy its delicious taste.

    Gresik specialties above really arouse your appetite. From every explanation food, you already know that it is so delicious. All food in the city of Gresik is priced very much affordable. There may be some foods that are more expensive, such as food green shell seafood.

  2. Nasi Krawu

    3. Nasi Krawu Gresik is made from a special recipe. the ingredients for making krawu rice are rice, chili paste, shredded chicken, relish of grated coconut and spices, shrimp or fish paste condiment. can also add side dishes such as tofu, eel, tripe, intestines, fried lungs, and beef intestine stew. Gresik krawu rice is more special because more choices of side dishes.

  3. Otak-Otak Bandeng

    4. otak-otak bandeng brains are not only in the city of Sidoarjo, but also in the city of Gresik. The tourists really like otak-otak bandeng brains in Gresik because they taste so delicious. Most tourists also buy otak-otak bandeng brains to make souvenirs.

    This food is very delicious and its safety is guaranteed, because the people of Gresik produce their own otak-otak bandeng brains without any preservatives.

  4. Green Shell Seafood

    5. the next typical Gresik food is green shell seafood. You will definitely find a variety of green clam dishes. Unfortunately, this green shellfish dish is only sold at seafood restaurants, you must visit a seafood restaurant if you want this food.

  5. Jubung

    6. Jubung is a typical Gresik food made from black sticky rice. The top of the veil sprinkled with sesame seeds. It should be noted that jubung is a snack food that already exists in various cities, however, the typical Gresik jubung tastes better. The veil is wrapped using the bark from the areca tree, so that the appearance of the jubung looks very special.

    Lots of jubung sellers that can be found along the streets of Gresik.
